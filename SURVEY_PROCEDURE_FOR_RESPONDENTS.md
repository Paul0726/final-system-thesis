# SURVEY PROCEDURE FOR RESPONDENTS
## BSIT Graduate Tracer System

---

## Overview

This document provides step-by-step instructions for BSIT graduates on how to complete the online tracer survey. The survey collects information about your educational background, employment status, and feedback about the BSIT program.

---

## Prerequisites

Before starting the survey, please prepare the following information:

- **Personal Information:**
  - Full name
  - Permanent address
  - Mobile number(s)
  - Email address
  - Date of birth
  - Current location/address

- **Educational Information:**
  - Course graduated (BSIT, BSIT Multimedia, BSIT Animation, etc.)
  - School year graduated
  - Academic achievements
  - Training and seminars attended
  - Professional licenses and certifications
  - Professional organization memberships

- **Employment Information:**
  - Current employment status
  - Job title and place of work (if employed)
  - Monthly income range
  - Additional revenue sources (if any)

---

## Step-by-Step Procedure

### STEP 1: Access the Survey System

1. Open your web browser (Google Chrome, Firefox, Microsoft Edge, or Safari)
2. Go to the BSIT Graduate Tracer System website
3. You will see the **Landing Page** with system information

### STEP 2: Start the Survey

1. On the Landing Page, click the **"Start Survey Now"** button
2. A verification modal will appear asking if you are a BSIT graduate
3. Click **"Yes, I am a BSIT Graduate"** to proceed
4. You will be redirected to the **Survey Page**

### STEP 3: Fill Out Section A - Individual Information

1. **Full Name:** Enter your complete name (First Name, Middle Name, Last Name)
2. **Permanent Address:** Enter your permanent home address
3. **Mobile Number:** Enter your primary mobile number
4. **Additional Mobile Number (Optional):** Enter another mobile number if you have one
5. **Email Address:** Enter your active email address
6. **Date of Birth:** Select your birth date from the calendar
7. **Age:** Your age will be automatically calculated
8. **Civil Status:** Select your current civil status (Single, Married, Widowed, Separated)
9. **Sex:** Select your sex (Male, Female)
10. **Current Location/Address:** Enter your current address if different from permanent address

**Note:** Fields marked with asterisk (*) are required and must be filled out.

### STEP 4: Fill Out Section B - Educational Background

1. **Course Graduated:** Select your course from the dropdown menu
   - Options: BSIT, BSIT Multimedia, BSIT Animation, etc.

2. **School Year Graduated:** Select the academic year you graduated
   - Format: YYYY-YYYY (e.g., 2020-2021)

3. **Maximum Academic Achievement:** Select your highest academic achievement
   - Options: Cum Laude, Magna Cum Laude, Summa Cum Laude, or None

4. **Training and Seminars Attended:** 
   - Click **"Add Training"** button
   - Enter the training/seminar name
   - Enter the date attended
   - Click **"Add"** to save
   - Repeat for additional trainings

5. **Professional Licenses and Certifications:**
   - Click **"Add License/Certification"** button
   - Enter the license/certification name
   - Enter the date obtained
   - Click **"Add"** to save
   - Repeat for additional licenses/certifications

6. **Professional Organization Memberships:**
   - Click **"Add Organization"** button
   - Enter the organization name
   - Enter the date joined
   - Click **"Add"** to save
   - Repeat for additional organizations

### STEP 5: Fill Out Section C - Employment Experiences

1. **Is Employed?** Select your current employment status
   - Options: Yes, No

2. **If Employed, select Employment Nature:**
   - Options: Government Sector, Private Sector, Self-Employed, Further Studies, Not Currently Employed

3. **Employment Classification:** Select your employment classification
   - Options: Regular/Permanent, Contractual, Part-time, Project-based, Others

4. **Job Title:** Enter your current job title or position

5. **Place of Work:** Enter the name of your company or organization

6. **Is your work in the IT (Information Technology) field?** 
   - Select: **Yes** if your current work is related to Information Technology
   - Select: **No** if your current work is not related to Information Technology
   - **Note:** This field is required if you are employed

7. **Monthly Income:** Select your monthly income range from the dropdown
   - Options range from "Below 10k" to "100k plus and above"

8. **Additional Revenue Sources:** Enter any other sources of income (if applicable)
   - Leave blank if none

### STEP 6: Fill Out Section D - Ratings and Feedback

Rate your agreement with the following statements using a scale of 1 to 5:
- **1 = Disagree**
- **2 = Slightly Disagree**
- **3 = Neutral**
- **4 = Agree**
- **5 = Strongly Agree**

1. **Job Placement:**
   - Rate: "The BSIT program prepared me well for job placement"

2. **IT Field:**
   - Rate: "The BSIT program prepared me well for the IT field"

3. **Competitive Edge:**
   - Rate: "The BSIT program gave me a competitive edge in the job market"

4. **Workplace:**
   - Rate: "I am satisfied with my current workplace"

### STEP 7: Create User Account (Optional but Recommended)

1. After filling out all sections, you will see an option to create a user account
2. **Email:** Your email address will be pre-filled
3. **Password:** Create a strong password (minimum 6 characters)
4. **Confirm Password:** Re-enter your password to confirm
5. Click **"Create Account"** if you want to:
   - Access your survey data later
   - Edit or update your information
   - View your personal dashboard

**Note:** You can skip this step if you don't want to create an account, but creating an account allows you to update your information later.

### STEP 8: Review and Submit

1. **Review your answers** before submitting:
   - Scroll through all sections
   - Check that all required fields are filled
   - Verify that your information is correct

2. **Submit the Survey:**
   - Click the **"Submit Survey"** button at the bottom of the form
   - Wait for the submission to process (this may take a few seconds)

3. **Success Message:**
   - You will see a success message confirming that your survey has been submitted
   - If you created an account, you can click **"Go to Personal Dashboard"** to view your data
   - If you didn't create an account, you can click **"Return to Home"** to go back to the landing page

## Important Reminders

### ✅ Do's:

- **Fill out all required fields** (marked with asterisk *)
- **Provide accurate and truthful information**
- **Review your answers** before submitting
- **Create a user account** if you want to update your information later
- **Use a valid email address** for account creation and future communications
- **Complete the survey in one session** (it takes approximately 10-15 minutes)

### ❌ Don'ts:

- **Don't submit incomplete forms** - all required fields must be filled
- **Don't provide false information** - this affects the accuracy of the tracer study
- **Don't refresh the page** while filling out the form - you may lose your progress
- **Don't use special characters** in fields that don't require them
- **Don't skip the IT Field question** if you are currently employed

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: I can't see the "Start Survey Now" button
**Solution:** 
- Make sure you're using a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari)
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page

### Problem: The form won't submit
**Solution:**
- Check that all required fields (marked with *) are filled
- Verify that your email address is in the correct format
- Make sure your date of birth is valid
- Check your internet connection
- Try again after a few moments

### Problem: I forgot to create an account and want to edit my survey
**Solution:**
- Contact the system administrator
- Provide your email address used in the survey
- Request access to your survey data

### Problem: I'm having trouble on mobile device
**Solution:**
- The system is optimized for mobile devices
- Make sure you're using the latest version of your mobile browser
- Try rotating your device to landscape mode for better viewing
- Use a stable internet connection

### Problem: The page is loading slowly
**Solution:**
- Check your internet connection speed
- Close other browser tabs or applications
- Try using a different browser
- Wait a few moments and try again

## After Submission

### What Happens Next?

1. **Data Storage:** Your survey data is securely stored in the system database
2. **Data Analysis:** Your responses will be included in statistical analysis and reports
3. **Dashboard Updates:** Your data will contribute to the public dashboard statistics
4. **Account Access:** If you created an account, you can log in anytime to view or edit your information

### Accessing Your Personal Dashboard

If you created an account:

1. Go to the system website
2. Click **"Login"** or **"Personal Dashboard"**
3. Enter your email address and password
4. You will receive an OTP (One-Time Password) via email
5. Enter the OTP to access your personal dashboard
6. You can view and edit your survey information
