# Privacy Policy for BSIT Graduate Tracer System

## Privacy Statement

This Privacy Policy describes how the BSIT Graduate Tracer System ("we", "our", or "the system") collects, uses, and protects personal information submitted through our website at **dwcsjgraduatetracer.it.com**.

---

## 1. Information Collection

### 1.1 Personal Information Collected

The system collects the following personal information from BSIT graduates:

- **Individual Information:**
  - Full Name
  - Permanent Address
  - Mobile Number(s)
  - Email Address
  - Date of Birth
  - Age
  - Civil Status
  - Sex
  - Current Location or Address

- **Educational Information:**
  - Course Graduated
  - School Year Graduated
  - Maximum Academic Achievement
  - Training and Seminars Attended
  - Professional Licenses and Certifications
  - Professional Organization Memberships

- **Employment Information:**
  - Employment Status
  - Employment Nature
  - Job Title/Position
  - Place of Work
  - Monthly Income Range
  - Additional Revenue Sources

- **Survey Responses:**
  - Ratings and feedback regarding the BSIT program
  - Job placement experiences
  - Workplace relationships

---

## 2. Purpose of Data Collection

The personal information collected is used for the following purposes:

1. **Academic Research:** To conduct tracer studies and evaluate the effectiveness of the BSIT program
2. **Program Improvement:** To identify areas for curriculum enhancement and program development
3. **Statistical Analysis:** To generate reports and statistics about graduate employment and career outcomes
4. **Communication:** To contact graduates for follow-up surveys or program updates (if consent is given)

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Data Storage

- All data is stored securely on Railway cloud hosting platform
- Data is stored in an in-memory database for the duration of the research period
- Access to data is restricted to authorized administrators only

### 3.2 Security Measures

- Secure HTTPS connection for all data transmission
- Access controls and authentication for administrative functions
- Regular security updates and monitoring

---

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Confidentiality

- Personal information is treated with strict confidentiality
- Data is used solely for academic research purposes
- Individual responses are not disclosed to third parties without consent

### 4.2 Data Sharing

- Aggregated and anonymized data may be used in research publications
- No personally identifiable information is shared in public reports
- Data may be shared with authorized academic personnel for research purposes only

---

## 5. Data Retention

- Data will be retained for the duration of the research study
- After the research period, data may be archived or deleted according to institutional policies
- Graduates may request deletion of their data at any time

---

## 6. User Rights

Graduates have the right to:

1. **Access:** Request access to their personal information
2. **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate information
3. **Deletion:** Request deletion of their personal information
4. **Withdrawal:** Withdraw consent to participate in the survey at any time

---

## 7. Consent

By submitting the survey form, graduates consent to:

- The collection and processing of their personal information
- The use of their data for research and program improvement purposes
- The storage of their data in the system's database
