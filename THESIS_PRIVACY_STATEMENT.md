### **Privacy and Ethical Considerations**

**Data Privacy Compliance:**
This research study complies with the **Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173)** of the Philippines, which governs the collection, processing, and protection of personal information. The study also adheres to ethical guidelines for research involving human subjects.

**Informed Consent:**
Participants provide informed consent by voluntarily submitting the online survey form. The survey includes a clear statement of the research purpose, data usage, and participant rights. By completing and submitting the survey, participants acknowledge their understanding and consent to participate in the study.

**Data Collection:**
Personal information collected includes individual demographics, educational background, employment status, and program feedback. All data is collected through the BSIT Graduate Tracer System web application hosted at **dwcsjgraduatetracer.it.com**.

**Data Security:**
- Data is stored securely on Railway cloud hosting platform with industry-standard security measures
- All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS protocol
- Access to the database is restricted to authorized research personnel only
- Regular security updates and monitoring are implemented

**Confidentiality:**
Individual survey responses are treated with strict confidentiality. Personal information is not disclosed to third parties without explicit consent. Only aggregated and anonymized data is used in research publications and reports.

**Participant Rights:**
Participants have the right to:
- Access their personal information
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of their information
- Withdraw from the study at any time
